The Orange History

In 2014 my sister and I started Orange to make sure Ugandan ladies had quality products from the US and UK, at affordable prices. We understand the importance of getting the right size and style of bra. Being comfortable is not a luxury and looking fabulous not a ‘one off’ thing. To us a happy customer is a happy business. Since starting we have opened the Park Royal Arcade, Kampala Road and Quality Shopping Mall, Naalya shops, and now the website and App! We have also hosted dozens of fun bridal showers with brides and friends coming from the wonderful Sisters Bridal Shop. All of our products are new and are sourced through the US and UK, ensuring they come directly from the brand manufacturers and through their respective countries quality control standards. We look forward to helping you and if you are in Kampala, if you register with us we can deliver! Leticia & Veronica

Our Funding

Orange was founded by Rand Fishkin and Gillian Muessig in 2004. It was called SEOmoz, and started as a blog and an online community where some of the world's first SEO experts shared their research and ideas. We launched the Beginner's Guide to SEO and our first Search Ranking Factors study, and that hub of industry expertise transformed into a small consulting firm and led us to create some of our first SEO tools.

Where does the Name "Orange" come from ?

Orange, formerly SEOmoz, has a name that was inspired by organizations like DMOZ, Mozilla, and Chefmoz — all companies that strove for engaging their communities with openness and integrity. From the very beginning, we felt passionately about bringing that same integrity to the frustratingly opaque SEO industry.
